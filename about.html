Feelstora present for you, A simple and effective to do list tools for organizing and managing tasks. It is a list of tasks or activities that need to be completed within a specified timeframe. Feelstora can be customized based on your preferences and needs.

Simple Power To Do List!

Keep focus with us. All it takes are lists, notebook, and calendar to boost your productivity.

To Do List!

Use a “To Do List” to capture each powerful step of the project work. User can add task, due date, type of project, status, and description. You can also edit or delete your task. Start simple, customize as you go.

My Notebook

“My Notebook”, your space to organize and prioritize your projects. You can add task description, edit even delete your task. Capture important ideas and information in a way that helps you stay productive.

My Calendar

View your work on “My Calendar” to easily identify gaps and overlaps in your schedule and make adjustments. Simply create an event to your calendar and boost productivity for everyone.
